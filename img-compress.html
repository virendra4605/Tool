How to Compress Images Online

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = better quality)
  3. Select your desired output format (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  5. Download your optimized image with reduced file size

Why Compress Images?

Image compression helps:

  • Improve website loading speed and performance
  • Reduce bandwidth usage and hosting costs
  • Optimize images for social media and email
  • Save storage space on your devices
  • Meet website requirements for image uploads
